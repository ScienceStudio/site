24.02.2016 Why I want a textbook solutions videoblog site/channel.

Modern education is an underestimated wealth, which has to be really improved. One of main education process' parts are textbooks, which have tasks, problems and questions. Students have to resolve them by themselves, make points, sometimes right, sometimes wrong and then go to class, where teacher tells or discusses how it should really has to be solved. This is pure useless waste of teacher's and student's time because of many repetitons of one thing description, low number of opinions, no room for cricitism. There are a lot of problems, resolvement of which is hard to find despite obvious solutions (for example, if plane takes off on a lane, which is moving with the same speed as plane, but moving opposite direction; or try this one: ice cube is floating on water in glass. How will level of water change after melting of ice cube?).

Of course, there are answer services provided by some companies, but usually scientific answers are not good if it is not stackoverflow obviously. Modern blogging proved that everything can be learnt only by subscribing on youtube/twitch/vimeo/etc cahnnel and understood in 30 mins during breakfast or lunch or even dinner. Imagine, that once a day somebody can watch at least one solution of textbook problem, he would have heard about 365 tasks, which is not a lot, but it is 10% of mathematical analysis book (for example, book made by Demidovich). There are number of channels who are already making that. For example, I really like numberphile youtube channel.

So, I am starting this project for Russian mathematical analysis textbook made by Demidivich, and there is obvious question about copyright. Don't know answer yet. Stay tuned.
